Etheranians is a community-driven NFT project. A collection of limited memberships to a unique community and benefits. Created in the Ethereum blockchain, is a representation of the blockchain workers and habitants. We will be offering direct communication about NFT, crypto, design and many other fields through our discord. Having an Etheranian will allow you to participate in this historic moment and with the upcoming launch of Ethereum 2.0, they will be available to be minted soon in 2022.

They are made up with over 2000 layers, creating unique combinations of Metaverse inspired traits of faces, tribes, accessories, gear and backgrounds. It’s a profitable Asset, that generates ETH, and while you hold it, it allows you to be a part of every community benefit.

1. GENERATIVE PHASE

Etheranians team is taking the time to carefully design each of the elements necessary to build the entire collection. On the launch date, a special script will be used to combine all the variables and randomly generate 4,869 (Ethereum ATH to date) of unique Etheranians with attributes from different rarities. This will create a very fun-to-trade NFT collection. The Mint Phase will be available on our website on the Ethereum Blockchain and with a Metamask wallet integration. Public sales will be open in 2022.

2. GRAND REVEAL

Once the minting phase is over, all the NFTs will be revealed and the only way to purchase Etheranians will be on the secondary marketplaces like OpenSea. All the OG buyers will be able to claim a pixelated version of their NFTs. Holders will get a special surprise. To qualify as a Holder you need to keep your Etheranians for at least 1week after the reveal date. The grand reveal will allow you to see all the minted Etheranians as well as yours, followed by a public rarity chart, that way everyone will know how to trade them.

3. FREQUENT RAFFLES, GIVEAWAYS AND FUN EVENTS

In order to celebrate the success of our project, we plan on doing frequent giveaways and raffles in which all of our community members can participate. We will offer events through social media where you will gain the opportunity to win Etheranians, ETH and even a customized NFT. Also, for the community there will be physical events like scavenger hunts and more. There will be exclusive giveaways for Holders, with many prizes every time that Ethereum reaches a new +20% ATH.

4. GENERATION 2.0

In 2022, with the official release of Ethereum 2.0, we plan on launching Generation 2.0. The concept of this new generation will be revealed in the future with many new and exciting benefits for our beloved community… Stay tuned!

5. BRIDGE TO THE METAVERSE AND THE FUTURE OF ETHERANIANS

One goal we desire to achieve in the not so long term is to be part of the Metaverse. With our community as the center of our project, our goal is to expand from a PFP project and provide a real utility in the Metaverse. By fusing the real world with the Metaverse, Etheranians Holders will benefit from exclusive merch ranging from virtual wearables to physical products such as 3D printed toys, clothing and frames. Etheranians are ment to be much more than an NFT. We have plans and hopes to build a huge interactive community that’s constantly growing and we wish to find collaboration with brands and other projects! We want the community to help dictate and determine which features we should be focusing on in the future, so you guys can feel as a part of the Etheranians team! Inspired by artists like Beeple, Kaws, Takashi Murakami and many more as well as other great NFT projects like Doodles, Weird Whales and BAYC, we look forward to becoming an icon in Pop art culture, participating in events like ComplexCon, receiving shoutouts from Hypebeast and appearing on NY Times Square.

Apart from owning original artwork that could increase a lot in value and being able to use it as a profile picture, you can become a part of the team and build the future of Etheranians. Besides all the benefits mentioned before, Holders will be granted the exclusive benefit of participating in polls through our forums to help us guide the direction of the project. We will need the help of all our users to determine specifics for future developments like Generation 2.0, utilities, applications and more! We’re giving back to the community 20% of all ETH raised (through reinvesting in publicity, making contests, giveaways, raffles, and special events) because we genuinely want Etheranians to be a happy-community driven project.

That will be ultimately up to you. We believe Etheranians are here to stay with a long life ahead of them and it will be an ever growing and evolving project. However its success relies not only on the work of the team but also in the participation of the whole community.
